FROM SIGNAL TO TRADE

Agents that trade on evidence, not vibes.

A research agent scans on-chain wallet flows, social signals, and market feeds surfacing a ranked watchlist of tokens worth looking at. It scores every candidate across seven dimensions: wallet flow, liquidity quality, volume momentum, social velocity, holder distribution, news catalysts, and contract safety.

When a candidate passes the score threshold, it's handed to a trading agent that backtests it against multiple strategies. Only tokens that clear the regression gate get promoted. Everything else is discarded. No overfitting. No hope trades.

Discover → Score → Backtest → Execute
Every token earns its way in: discovered, scored, stress-tested, or rejected.

Two agents run a structured loop: one discovers candidates, the other backtests and only promotes what clears gates. Research briefs move through a typed handoff contract, and execution defaults to paper mode. Live mode stays off until explicit approval.

Strategy inference is routed through Speakeasy for privacy-preserving evaluation and multi-model robustness testing. Strategy research, deep reasoning, and refinement are tested across multiple model routes (including uncensored lanes where appropriate) before promotion. Interactive chat or MCP sessions can still use the user's chosen base model/runtime.

The system is fail-safe by design: confidence tiers degrade automatically as data quality falls. Everything is open source under Apache-2.0 so you can inspect strategies, gates, adapters, and control flow end-to-end.
