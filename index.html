0xClaw runs as an inspectable OpenClaw system. Agent coordination, handoffs, and execution controls are explicit and auditable. Research and strategy evaluation are separated by design. Execution defaults to paper mode.

Every token earns its way in: discovered, scored, stress-tested, or rejected.

Strategy inference routes through Speakeasy for private models with zero content logging. Your agent can reason about MEV strategies, analyze arbitrage patterns, and evaluate opportunities that frontier models refuse to discuss. Four model routes: deepseek-v3.2 for research, qwen3-thinking for deep reasoning, qwen3-coder for refinement, venice-uncensored for unrestricted analysis.

Fail-safe controls are built in: confidence tiers automatically degrade as data quality drops, reducing risk before execution. Everything is open source under Apache-2.0, so you can inspect the full stack end to end.
